Server Rules

Play fair, respect others, and keep RP immersive. Breaking rules can lead to warnings, kicks, or bans.

General Rules

  • Follow staff instructions at all times. Arguing in the moment = removal; appeal after.
  • No harassment, hate speech, slurs, doxxing, or threats (IC or OOC).
  • No ERP/sexually explicit content or NSFW emotes.
  • Respect Value of Life — act as if life has real consequences.
  • No griefing/trolling/stream sniping.
  • No combat logging or force respawning to avoid RP.
  • Stay updated in Discord for rule changes & announcements.

Engagement Standards

  • No cop-baiting/provoking law enforcement for attention.
  • Give clear initiation before violence (verbal or obvious threat in RP).
  • If downed, do not return or rejoin the same active scene.
  • EMS/PD scenes must be respected; do not block/interrupt medical RP.
  • Crime cooldowns apply server-wide; don’t chain robberies back-to-back.

OOC Rules

  • Keep OOC chat to Discord; avoid breaking immersion in-game.
  • No metagaming: don’t use stream/Discord info IC.
  • Powergaming is forbidden (forcing outcomes; abusing menus/animations/physics).
  • Respect safe spaces (tutorial spawn, job start locations) — no hostile RP there.

Communication Rules

  • Use clear English in citywide/scene communications (other languages okay within your group if all agree).
  • Radio use must be realistic (no magical info sharing across scenes).
  • No ear-rape/voice changers that harm clarity; keep music to private spaces.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No external cheats, macro abuse, or crosshair overlays.
  • No bug abuse/dupes (stash glitches, teleport exploits, invisible spots, etc.). Report issues to staff.
  • Cosmetic client mods only (no gameplay advantage).

Roleplay Rules

  • No RDM/VDM: Random Deathmatch & Vehicle Deathmatch are bannable.
  • NLR: After respawn/hospital, don’t return/interfere with the same scene for 30 minutes.
  • Hostage RP must be reasonable; no fake hostages or impossible demands.
  • Major robberies require a plan & negotiation. Pure “drive & shoot” with no RP is LQRP.
  • Do not camp hospitals/PD/robberies for cheap kills or loot.
  • Military bases/aircraft carriers are restricted unless a staff-run event says otherwise.

Character Guidelines

  • Use realistic names (no memes/celebs). Characters must be 18+.
  • Keep characters separate. No memory/info sharing between your own characters.
  • Government roles (PD/EMS/DOJ) have stricter conduct; corruption needs prior staff approval.
  • Properties/vehicles may be reclaimed after long inactivity to keep the city fresh.
