Server Rules

General Rules

  • Stay updated with all rule changes via our Discord.
  • No erotic roleplay or sexually explicit content.
  • Zero tolerance for racism, trolling, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Avoid real-life tragic topics (suicide, terrorism, pandemics, etc.) in RP.

Engagement Standards

  • Avoid cop baiting or provoking law enforcement without proper RP context.
  • Focus on meaningful roleplay and interactions, not money grinding.
  • Cooperate with EMS in medical scenarios and respect their role.

OOC Rules

  • Keep OOC discussions to Discord channels only.
  • Avoid immersion-breaking language, sounds, or behavior.

Communication Rules

  • English is required for clear communication in roleplay.
  • Always treat staff and fellow players with respect.
  • Excessive volume/toxicity over voice may be moderated by staff.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No unfair modifications or cheats that give any advantage.
  • Visual mods are allowed if they don’t affect balance.
  • Report bugs; exploiting bugs will result in bans and wipes of gains.

Roleplay Rules

  • Restricted areas (e.g., Fort Zancudo, carriers) are off-limits unless authorized.
  • New Life Rule: 30-minute cooldown before returning to the scene after being downed.
  • No RDM/VDM; always initiate RP and communicate intentions.

Character Guidelines

  • Maintain professionalism in official roles (LEO/EMS/DOJ/DOC).
  • Keep characters’ knowledge and storylines separate.
  • Long-term inactivity can lead to property removal to keep the world active.
