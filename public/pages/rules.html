Server Rules

View Detailed Rulebook

General Rules

Stay Updated: Regularly check our Discord for any updates or modifications to the rules.

No Erotic Roleplay: Explicit or sexually suggestive content is strictly prohibited.

Zero Tolerance Policy: Racism, trolling, discrimination, homophobia, and sexism are not tolerated.

Engagement Standards

Cop Baiting: Intentionally provoking or luring police officers without proper roleplay is prohibited.

Money Simulator Gameplay: Focus on meaningful interactions rather than just accumulating wealth.

OOC Rules

OOC Discussions: Conduct out-of-character discussions in Discord channels only.

Immersion: Avoid using immersion-breaking language.

Communication Rules

Language Requirements: Communication must be in English.

Respect for Staff: Maintain respectful interactions with staff members.

Cheating and Exploiting

Prohibition of Unfair Modifications: No game modifications that provide unfair advantages.

Bug Exploitation: Exploiting bugs is strictly prohibited.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas: Fort Zancudo and aircraft carriers are restricted.

New Life Rule: Follow the 30-minute cooldown after incapacitation.

Character Guidelines

Integrity for Official Roles: Maintain professional conduct in official positions.

Character Separation: Keep characters distinct and independent.
