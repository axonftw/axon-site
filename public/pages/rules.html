Server Rules

General Rules

  • Stay updated with all rule changes via our Discord.
  • No erotic roleplay or sexually explicit content.
  • Zero tolerance for racism, trolling, or discrimination of any kind.

Engagement Standards

  • Avoid cop baiting or provoking law enforcement without proper RP context.
  • Roleplay for meaningful interaction, not just wealth accumulation.

OOC Rules

  • Keep OOC discussions to Discord, not in-game chat.
  • Avoid immersion-breaking language or actions.

Communication Rules

  • English is required for clear communication in roleplay.
  • Always treat staff and fellow players with respect.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No unfair modifications or cheats that give you an advantage.
  • Bug exploitation is strictly prohibited — report bugs instead.

Roleplay Rules

  • Restricted areas like Fort Zancudo are off-limits unless authorized.
  • Follow New Life Rule: 30-minute cooldown after incapacitation.

Character Guidelines

  • Maintain professionalism in official roles.
  • Keep your characters’ stories separate and consistent.
