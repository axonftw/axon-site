About This Server

Purpose

Welcome to AXV3 RP, a custom-built FiveM roleplay server designed for serious players who value realism, quality, and community.

Our goal is to provide a next-generation RP experience with unique scripts, immersive scenarios, and an active, supportive player base.

Mission

Make high-quality, immersive FiveM roleplay accessible to everyone.

Vision

Build a trusted and lasting RP community where creativity, fairness, and fun come first, powered by passionate players and dedicated staff.
