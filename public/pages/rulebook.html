Server Rulebook

All AXV3 rules — same layout and polish as the rest of the site.

General Rules

Server Guidelines

Stay Updated

Regularly check our Discord for any updates or modifications to the rules. It's your responsibility to stay informed about any changes that may affect your gameplay.

No Erotic Roleplay

Explicit or sexually suggestive content, including nudity or lewd materials, is strictly prohibited. This extends to all forms of ERP to maintain a professional and respectful environment.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Racism, trolling, discrimination, homophobia, and sexism are not tolerated. We enforce a strict policy against any form of hate speech or discriminatory behavior.

Sensitive Topics

Real-life events such as suicide, terrorism, pandemics, and mental illnesses are not permitted in roleplay. We prioritize the well-being and safety of our community members.

Engagement Standards

Player Engagement

Cop Baiting

Intentionally provoking or luring police officers into scenarios without proper roleplay is considered LQRP. Examples include deliberately colliding with police cars to force a chase.

Money Simulator Gameplay

Focus on meaningful interactions rather than just accumulating wealth. Build relationships and create dynamic scenarios that extend beyond finances.

Cooperation with EMS

Refusing to respond to or cooperate with EMS staff can lead to consequences. Engage with EMS as part of realistic roleplay.

OOC Rules

Out of Character Guidelines

  • Conduct out-of-character discussions only in Discord channels.

  • Avoid using immersion-breaking language.

Communication Rules

Communication Guidelines

Language Requirements

Communication must be in English. Other languages are okay if all parties agree, but English is the server standard.

Respect for Staff

Maintain respectful interactions with staff. Combative behavior or creating issues may result in removal.

Voice Communication

Excessively loud or targeted offensive language is not tolerated. Staff may address misuse of in-character exceptions.

Cheating & Exploiting

Anti-Cheat Guidelines

Unfair Modifications

Mods that provide unfair advantages (speed hacks, regen, teleport, etc.) are strictly prohibited.

Permissible Mods

Purely visual enhancements are allowed. Key binds to server commands are fine if they don’t grant advantages.

Bug Exploitation

Abusing client/server bugs is strictly prohibited and will result in bans and removal of gains.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo and aircraft carriers are off-limits due to heavy security.

Deathmatch, Revenge, KOS

  • LQRP: Intentionally running over individuals can still be LQRP even if “justified”; focus on immersion.
  • Self-Defense: You may respond if attacked or property is harmed. Keep responses proportional and roleplayed.
  • RDM: Never harm/kill without valid IC reason & proper roleplay.
  • VDM: Never use vehicles to harm without valid IC justification (and even then, it may be LQRP).
  • Initiation: Before any shootout or violent action, initiate roleplay and communicate intentions.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Trigger: When you respawn or are treated at hospital after being incapacitated.
  • Return: Wait 30 minutes before returning to the scene. No looting/evidence collection.
  • Hostility: No hostile actions against the responsible party for 60 minutes.
  • Closed Scenes: Don’t return to concluded scenarios.

Combat RP Logging

  • Do not disconnect/respawn to avoid IC consequences.
  • If you crash, try to reconnect and resume the scene.
  • Do not use emotes to gain unfair combat advantage.

Metagaming, Powergaming, Torture RP

  • Metagaming: Using OOC info for IC advantage is a serious offense.
  • Streaming: Players in the city may not appear in others’ streams who are also in the city.
  • Powergaming: Don’t roleplay nonexistent abilities or force outcomes with no choices. (e.g., spam jumping, unfair cuff/escort, flipping cars, etc.)
  • Vehicles: No vehicles with unbreakable/tiny/covered windows or unfair advantages for crime.
  • Torture RP: Requires explicit consent from all parties via /me; stop immediately if anyone is uncomfortable.

Prison Roleplay

  • Lifers may not break out without staff approval (open a ticket).
  • Always roleplay as if DOC is present, even if none are online.
  • Breaking in without a valid plan is LQRP. Don’t use teleports unless you’re an active prisoner.
  • EMS may transport inmates for required medical procedures.

Private Discords

  • Any AXV3-related Discords with IC channels must follow AXV3 rules.
  • Clearly label which channels are in-character.
  • Businesses/gangs must post invite links in the main AXV3 Discord.
  • Staff may request access for compliance; refusal can lead to action.
  • Provide accurate evidence to staff when requested.

Criminal Activities

  • 5-Man Rule: Max five participants until the scenario is resolved; no outside interference.
  • Mechanics: May not use job powers during crime scenes.
  • Groups: Separate groups may not “combine” objectives during the same crime.
  • Prison Transports: Treated as a new scenario; you may assemble a fresh group of up to five.
  • No Fake Hostages.
  • Major Robberies: Require real planning; simple evasion is not enough.
  • Robbing Players: Allowed with high-quality RP and justified context. Focus on RP quality.
  • Cooldowns: Store/Car Boosts: 45m • Vangelico: 90m • Fleeca/Trucks/Drugs: 120m • G6/Paleto/Pacific: 180m.
  • Teleport Areas: No robbing where “teleport” options exist.
  • Scams: Must be in-game RP with valid reason; no exploiting menus/teleports (except F2 inventory).
  • Gov Employees: Don’t rob PD/DOJ/EMS purely for belongings.
  • Interference: Only if on turf or if store owners intervene; not for revenge.
  • Restart Windows: No major crimes 60m before / 30m after restart; all crime banned 30m before/after restart.
  • Shooting Cooldown: 90 minutes for all scenarios.
  • Camping: Don’t camp/stake out crime spots. Check, then leave for 5 minutes before returning.
  • Unscheduled Restarts: Wait 15 minutes after a crash/restart before resuming crime.

Character Guidelines

Character Development

Integrity for Official Roles

Police, DOC, DOJ, and EMS must maintain professionalism and avoid corruption or misuse of equipment.

Character Separation

Each character operates independently with no cross-knowledge outside established RP.

Property Management

Properties may be removed after 90 days of inactivity. Sporadic logins to bypass this will not prevent removal.
