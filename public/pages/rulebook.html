Server Rules

The complete AXV3 rulebook.

General Rules Engagement OOC Rules Communication Cheating Roleplay Character

General Rules

  • Stay updated with all rule changes via our Discord.
  • No erotic roleplay or sexually explicit content.
  • Zero tolerance for racism, trolling, or discrimination of any kind.
  • Avoid using real-life tragic topics (suicide, terrorism, pandemics, etc.) in RP.

Engagement Standards

  • Avoid cop baiting or provoking law enforcement without proper RP context.
  • Roleplay for meaningful interaction, not just wealth accumulation.
  • Cooperate with EMS during medical scenarios.

OOC Rules

  • Keep OOC discussions in Discord channels only.
  • Do not break immersion with OOC talk in-city.

Communication Rules

  • Primary language is English (others only if all participants agree).
  • Maintain respectful interactions with staff.
  • No excessively loud/offensive voice use toward players.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No unfair modifications (speed, regen, teleport, etc.).
  • Visual-only mods that don’t affect balance are allowed.
  • No bug exploitation — gains will be removed and bans issued.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo and the aircraft carriers are restricted due to heavy security.

Deathmatch, Revenge & Kill On Sight

  • Vehicle ramming is generally LQRP even if “justified”.
  • Self-defense must be proportional and properly roleplayed.
  • RDM/VDM is strictly prohibited.
  • Always initiate RP and clearly communicate before violence.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Triggered on respawn or hospital treatment.
  • Wait 30 minutes before returning to the scene; 60 minutes before hostility.
  • Do not return to concluded scenes.

Combat Logging

  • No disconnect/respawn to avoid consequences.
  • If you crash, reconnect and resume the scenario.
  • No emote abuse for combat advantages.

Character Guidelines

  • PD/DOC/DOJ/EMS roles require integrity (no corruption/equipment abuse).
  • Keep characters separate; no cross-knowledge between alts.
  • Properties inactive for 90 days may be reclaimed.
