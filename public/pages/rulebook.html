Server Rulebook

All the AXV3 rules in one clean page.

General Engagement OOC Communication Cheating Roleplay Character

General Rules

  • Stay updated via our Discord announcements.
  • No erotic roleplay or explicit content.
  • Zero tolerance for racism, trolling, or discrimination.
  • Avoid real-life tragic topics (suicide, terrorism, pandemics, etc.) in RP.

Engagement Standards

  • No cop-baiting or provoking LEOs without proper RP.
  • Focus on meaningful RP, not money grinding.
  • Cooperate with EMS during medical scenarios.

OOC Rules

  • Keep OOC discussions in Discord channels only.
  • Avoid immersion-breaking language.

Communication Rules

  • Primary language: English (others only if all parties agree).
  • Be respectful to staff; hostile behavior can lead to removal.
  • No excessively loud/offensive voice comms.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No unfair mods (speed, regen, teleport, etc.).
  • Visual mods are fine if they donâ€™t affect balance.
  • Exploiting bugs leads to bans; gained benefits removed.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo and aircraft carriers are restricted due to heavy security.

Deathmatch / Revenge / KOS

  • Running people over is almost always LQRP.
  • Self-defense must match context and be roleplayed.
  • RDM/VDM is strictly prohibited.
  • Initiate RP and communicate before any shootout.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Triggers when you respawn or are treated at hospital.
  • Wait 30 minutes before returning to the scene.
  • No hostile actions against the responsible party for 60 minutes.

Combat / Logging

  • No disconnecting/respawning to avoid consequences.
  • If you crash, try to reconnect and continue RP.
  • No emote abuse to gain combat advantages.

Character Guidelines

  • Official roles (PD/DOC/DOJ/EMS) require integrity & professionalism.
  • Keep characters separate; no cross-knowledge.
  • Properties may be removed after 90 days of inactivity.
