General Rules

Server Guidelines

Stay Updated

Regularly check our Discord for any updates or modifications to the rules. It’s your responsibility to stay informed.

No Erotic Roleplay

ERP or sexually explicit content is strictly prohibited to maintain a respectful environment.

Zero Tolerance Policy

Racism, trolling, discrimination, homophobia, and sexism are not tolerated.

Sensitive Topics

Real-life tragedies (suicide, terrorism, pandemics, etc.) are not permitted in roleplay scenarios.

Engagement Standards

Player Engagement

Cop Baiting

Provoking police without proper RP is LQRP (e.g., intentionally colliding to force a chase).

Money Simulator

Focus on meaningful RP interactions—not just grinding cash.

Cooperate with EMS

Refusing to engage with EMS is poor RP and may carry consequences.

OOC Rules

Out of Character Guidelines

  • Keep OOC talk to Discord only.

  • Avoid immersion-breaking language.

Communication Rules

Communication Guidelines

Language

Primary language is English. Others allowed if all participants are comfortable.

Respect Staff

Keep interactions with staff respectful. Hostile behavior may lead to removal.

Voice

Excessively loud/offensive language at individuals is not tolerated; staff can intervene.

Cheating & Exploiting

Anti-Cheat Guidelines

Unfair Mods

No speed hacks, teleports, regen, etc.

Allowed Mods

Purely visual tweaks or keybinds that don’t give advantages are okay.

Bug Exploits

Abusing bugs is bannable; gains will be removed.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo and aircraft carriers are restricted due to heavy security.

Deathmatch, Revenge, KOS

  • LQRP: Using vehicles to run over people—even “justified”—is often LQRP. Keep RP quality high.
  • Self-Defense: Allowed when reasonably threatened; keep responses proportional and roleplayed.
  • RDM: Killing/harming without valid in-character reasoning is prohibited.
  • VDM: Using vehicles to kill/harm without proper RP is prohibited.
  • Initiation: Before violence, clearly initiate RP and communicate intentions.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Trigger: On respawn or hospital treatment.
  • Return: Wait 30 minutes before returning to the scene.
  • Hostility: No hostile actions for 60 minutes against the party responsible.
  • After Scenario: Don’t return to concluded scenes.

Combat Logging

  • No disconnect/respawn to avoid consequences.
  • If you crash, try to reconnect and resume.
  • No emote abuse for unfair advantages.

Metagaming / Powergaming / Torture RP

  • Metagaming: No OOC info used IC.
  • Streaming: Avoid appearing in other live streams from the same city.
  • Powergaming: Don’t force outcomes or use non-existent mechanics (spam jump, exploit stashes, unfair cuffing, etc.).
  • Vehicles: No vehicles with unfair window/visibility advantages for crime.
  • Torture RP: Requires prior consent via /me; stop immediately if discomfort is expressed.

Prison RP

  • Lifers can’t break out without staff approval.
  • RP as if DOC is present even if none online.
  • No abusing teleports to exit.
  • EMS may transport for necessary procedures.

Private Discords

  • IC channels must follow server rules; label IC clearly.
  • Groups share invite links in the main Discord.
  • Staff may request access for compliance checks.
  • Provide accurate evidence if staff requests it.

Crime & Gangs

  • Use gang gear only if earned IC.
  • Diversify gang RP beyond constant violence.
  • Always initiate RP verbally before combat.

Criminal Activities

  • 5-Man Rule: Max 5 until scenario ends; no outside interference.
  • No job ability abuse (e.g., mechanics F1) during crime.
  • Prison transport attacks are separate; new team of up to 5 allowed.
  • No fake hostages.
  • Major robberies require a real plan—no pure “drive to win”.
  • Robbing players allowed with solid RP and proper commands.
  • Cooldowns (examples): Stores/Boosts 45m • Vangelico 90m • Fleeca/Trucks/Drugs 120m • G6/Paleto/Pacific 180m.
  • No robbing in teleport-enabled areas.
  • No scamming outside proper RP or via exploits.
  • No robbing PD/DOJ/EMS just for items.
  • No interference in robberies unless turf/store-owner cases.
  • No major crimes 60m before / 30m after restart. All crime banned 30m before/after restart.
  • Shooting cooldown: 90m for all scenarios.
  • No camping crime spots; leave 5m before re-checking.
  • After unscheduled restart/crash: wait 15m before resuming crime.

Character Guidelines

Character Development

Integrity in Official Roles

Police/DOC/DOJ/EMS must keep high integrity; no corruption or equipment abuse.

Character Separation

Each character acts independently—no cross-knowledge between your alts.

Property Management

Properties may be removed after 90 days of inactivity to keep the world dynamic.
