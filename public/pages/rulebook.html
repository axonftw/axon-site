General Rules

Server Guidelines

  • Stay updated via Discord announcements.
  • No ERP or sexually explicit content.
  • Zero tolerance for racism, trolling, or discrimination.
  • Avoid real-life tragic topics (suicide, terrorism, pandemics, etc.) in RP.

Engagement Standards

Player Engagement

  • No cop-baiting or provoking LEOs without proper RP.
  • Prioritize meaningful interaction over money grinding.
  • Cooperate with EMS during medical scenarios.

OOC Rules

  • OOC chats stay in Discord only.
  • Don’t break immersion with OOC talk in-city.

Communication Rules

  • Primary language is English; others only if all agree.
  • Respect staff at all times.
  • No excessively loud/offensive voice use at players.

Cheating & Exploiting

  • No unfair mods (speed, regen, TP, etc.).
  • Cosmetic/visual mods that don’t affect balance are fine.
  • No bug exploitation; gains will be wiped + bans issued.

Roleplay Rules

Off-Limits Areas

Fort Zancudo & the aircraft carriers are restricted due to heavy security.

Deathmatch, Revenge & KOS

  • Vehicle ramming is generally LQRP.
  • Self-defense must be proportional and roleplayed.
  • RDM/VDM strictly prohibited.
  • Always initiate RP and communicate before violence.

New Life Rule (NLR)

  • Triggered on respawn or hospital treatment.
  • Wait 30m before returning to the scene; 60m before hostility.
  • Don’t return to concluded scenes.

Combat Logging

  • No disconnect/respawn to avoid consequences.
  • If you crash, reconnect and continue the scene.
  • No emote abuse to gain combat advantage.

Character Guidelines

  • Official roles (PD/DOC/DOJ/EMS) require high integrity; no corruption/equipment abuse.
  • Keep characters separate; no cross-knowledge between alts.
  • Inactive properties (90 days) may be reclaimed.
